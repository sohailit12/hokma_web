About Us

At HOKMA Computer Center, we provide specialized computer education and services to help you master IT skills and advance your career. Our experienced instructors and state-of-the-art facilities make learning both effective and enjoyable.

The future talent of the 21st century should be able to recognize and lead the future industry by not only using and handling existing technologies, but also demonstrating their capabilities as experts. In response to the ever-advancing technology and the demands of the times, our center trains and trains workers in the fields of computer and beauty. We always support the challenges of Pakistan south. It will be further developed by those who prepare the future of each area. Our HOKMA FTTC was stablished in January 2019 and trains more than 100 IT and beauty talents every year. We are doing our best today to train people who have both creativity and wisdom.
